Who are we

Meet the "Infinite Loopers," a dynamic team of four passionate web developers: Dhanabal, Jothiksha, Vaishnavi, and Junaidh Ahamed. Together, they have channeled their love for anime into creating a dedicated anime streaming website. Leveraging their skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, they've built a platform that not only showcases their technical prowess but also serves as a hub for fellow anime enthusiasts to explore and enjoy a vast collection of anime content. Whether you're in search of the latest releases or the cherished classics, the Infinite Loopers have curated a site that promises a seamless viewing experience for all anime fans.
